Frequently Asked Questions

What courses do you offer?

We offer a variety of courses in programming, web development, data science, and more. Check out our Courses page for more details.

How can I register for a course?

You can register for a course by visiting the Courses page and clicking on the "Learn More" button for the course you are interested in.

What is the refund policy?

Our refund policy allows you to request a full refund within the first 7 days of the course start date. For more information, please contact our support team.

Can I get a certificate after completing a course?

Yes, we provide a certificate of completion for all our courses. You can download your certificate from your account dashboard once you complete the course.
