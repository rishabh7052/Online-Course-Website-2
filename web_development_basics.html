Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any prior experience to take this course?

No prior experience is necessary. This course is designed for complete beginners.

What tools will I need for this course?

You will need a computer with an internet connection and a text editor such as Visual Studio Code.

How long will I have access to the course materials?

You will have lifetime access to the course materials, so you can review them at any time.

Is there a certificate upon completion?

Yes, you will receive a certificate of completion once you finish the course.
